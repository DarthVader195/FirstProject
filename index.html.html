Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2 c. red lentils
  • 1 T. Vegeta
  • salt and pepper as desired
  • Equivalent: soup pot, stirring spoon, sharp knife, grater

Steps

  1. Grate 2 carrots
  2. Finely dice a medium onion
  3. Saute the onion in a splotch of oil in the pot. Traditionally you'd add a tablespoon or two of tomato paste after sauteing.
  4. Add 7 cups of water, the carrots, and lentils.
  5. Season with Vegeta.
  6. Bring to a boil and then turn to the lowest simmer
  7. It will take about an hour for the lentils to cook

Notes: You can let it cook for as long as you like, but keep it on very low heat and stir whenever you walk past it.
